Biography

Bayerische Flugzeug-Werke made its debut in 1916, building aircraft engines. The name was later changed to "Bayerische Motoren Werke" in July 1917, shortened to "BMW". They started producing motorcycles in 1920, and dived in car business in 1928. During the second world war, the first jet airplane engines were build by BMW. After the war, BMW had difficulties and by 1959 almost went bankrupt because it couldn't compete in small-car market. In the same year, however, German entrepreneur Herbert Quandt got interested and BMW introduced the 700 series. After that BMW introduced the 1500 model and new series of motorcycles. BMW became very well established as premium automobile brand by 2000.
